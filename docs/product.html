Technology Spinoff¶

Perceive is a technology company started by three Purdue students (Kyle McNulty, Everett Berry, and Andrew Blejde) and Dr. Yung-Hsiang Lu in 2015. The company uses video analytics to understand human interactions in physical stores and improve customer service. Dr. Lu served as the scientific adviser and a member in the board between 2016/06-2019/05.
